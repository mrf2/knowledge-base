## Summary
 * The numbers `(1), (2), (11),...,(x)` are **TLS handshake message type IDs**
 * They are **defined by the TLS specification**
 * They are **1-byte numeric constants**
 * TLS 1.3 uses **fewer messages**, but keeps the same numbering
